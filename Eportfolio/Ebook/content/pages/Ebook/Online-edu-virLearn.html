Virtual Learning Environment

A virtual learning environment is short for (VLE) it is an educational platform which provides study materials, assignments, activities within the institutions. It is available for teacher and students in those institutions not for public users.

This way students are able to complete their assessments and submit from their comfort zone.

In VLE teachers have their own accounts with username and password. They have many features compared to the students because they have accessibility to update assessments, videos and study materials. However, student can log in to the VLE by providing their common username and password and allowed to look new updates.
