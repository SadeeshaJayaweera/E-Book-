The Digital Divide

Drawbacks

1. Student will neglect their school work and waste their time playing computer games and chatting

2. Finding an ICT job can be tough due to a large number of knowledgeable ICT workers.

3. It may affect to some cultures if the narrow is being down.

4. Electricity is a main requirement to use technology devices so people in third world countries have to pay big bill for the electricity.

5. People will be not socializing with the outside world if they are highly dependent on technology.

6. Health problems can occur because many people spend a lot of time in technology which will result in eyestrain or obesity.

7. Children will avoid playing physical games and only play computer games so it is not for the life of teenagers.
