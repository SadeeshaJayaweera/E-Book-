Advantages Of The Internet (Cont.)

Banking facility also available on the internet. people can make their transactions by making online banking account instead of visiting the bank branch. This service is available 24/7 so users can transfer money, pay bills, and a lot more. the account will be updated once a transaction is done.

People buy and sells using the internet. Majority of people shop through the internet because it is easy to find a product that is most relevant for them at a cheap price. And also many offers are available for regular customers. Items will be delivered to your doorstep so this saves the time and travel cost.

A lot of people spend their leisure time on the internet by playing online games and watching movies. Some call their friends to play games simultaneously.
