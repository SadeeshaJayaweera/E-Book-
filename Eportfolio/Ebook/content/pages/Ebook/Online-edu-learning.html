Education

Online Learning

Online learning is the point at which you take degree’s or diploma’s online rather than in a physical classroom. Online learning are normally divided into four fundamental parts: forum discussions, maintaining a personal portfolio, writing assignments, getting ready for tests, taking tests. You'll have to experience every one of these area’s so as to pass your course. A final mark is usually calculated at the end of the term over your performance.

To use this service, First you must find a website which offers online learning courses, and select a course that you preferred. Websites like Open_Yale_Courses,Stanford online,NovoEd,etc. Are examples for online learning websites. The students need to be register and make the required installment to get enrolled, Once this is done you will get a username and password for your online learning account. After logged in, user can have the study materials,pastpapers,etc…
