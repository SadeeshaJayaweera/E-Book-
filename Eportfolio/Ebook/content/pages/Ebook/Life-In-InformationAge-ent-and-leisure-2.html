Like good benefits, there is also some incidence in people’s life. When purchasing a ticket some failures can occur as there are some fakes sites which don’t provide tickets by taking users money.

Almost everyone in this world listens to music they like in their leisure hours. Sociologist’s state that “listening to music will reduce people's stress.” When comparing with past years most of the people listened to many songs but it is not much compared with today's society. I also listen to music while solving mathematical problems because it is easy for me to concentrate on the work. Majority of programmers in the global listen to “Techno beats” whilst they coding. As a result, music helps us in many ways.

People can listen to music in many ways in the present world rather than limiting to CDs or DVDs. There are many websites that provide music to people for free. But in earlier, musician earned money by issuing an album to their fans. Nowadays people download songs that they want legally or illegally. Legally download not cost money most of the time. Therefore, the majority of people tend to download music from the internet rather than purchasing music album CDS by paying a high amount of money.

“61% of people who buy CDs are 36 and older, according to Music Watch’s estimate. Ten years ago, that figure was just 36%. Back in 2004, people over 50 made up just 19% of the CD-buying population, but today they’re more than a third.” (statistics taken from “altpress.com”)

“As a group, 13 to 17-year-olds have plummeted to a mere 7% of the CD-buying population, down from 14.9% in 2004. Their share of digital purchases, either singles or albums, is also down a healthy 12 percentage points — to just 13% — as downloading has become more mainstream among older demographics.” (statistics taken from “altpress.com”)
