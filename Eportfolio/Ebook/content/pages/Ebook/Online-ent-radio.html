Entertainment

Online Radio

Online radio is an internet-based service which allows a user to listen to digital audio through the internet. However, this service is better compared to the traditional radio service, because traditional radio may be heard for 100 miles or very little further. Online radio stations don't have these restrictions, so you can tune into millions of radio stations any place you can get on the web.

Live365,TuneIn,IheartRadio,etc.Are examples for online radio.
