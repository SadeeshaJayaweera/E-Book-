Online Mapping and Route Plan

Google street view

Google street view is a tool which is available in the Google maps. This service provides 360 degree horizontal and 290 degree vertical panoramic pictures of various places in the world. Street view allows users to view their loved places in full resolution image.

This feature included in the Google maps, however user can use this service alternatively by downloading street view app or visiting their website using browser.

There is no need to have a such ICT skills to use this service, anyone can use it easily because this service has very user friendly interface.
