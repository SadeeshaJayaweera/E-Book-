Download Music

Download music is a procedure of downloading songs using the internet. There are many websites available on the internet, so user can use them to download the songs as they wish.

Most of the websites allow a user to download songs without registering into the website.

Jamendo,QTRAX,Amazon_mp3,etc.. Are good examples

And also there are many apps available for the smartphones, a user can download and listen to the songs by installing those apps into their mobile phone. "Google plays music app" can be taken as a good example for the smartphone apps.
