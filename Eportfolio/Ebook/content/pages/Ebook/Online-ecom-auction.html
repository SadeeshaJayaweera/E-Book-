E-Commerce

Online Auction

Online auction is an internet based service that enables people to bid or sell their products through the internet. During the auction,users can bid for products. At the end of the auction user with highest bid will win the product and purchase it by paying the money.

User can use this service by registering to an online auction site like e-Bid or uBid. After this is done. User able to bid for products. Every auction has a time period,If you hope to purchase a product then you must outbid other users during the end of the auction. Once this Is done you can have your product.
