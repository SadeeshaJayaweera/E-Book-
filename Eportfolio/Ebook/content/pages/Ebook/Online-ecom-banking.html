Online Banking

Online banking is known as e-banking. Which enables a user to manage their finance using their smartphone or computer. You can have an online banking account by visiting your bank branch, they will give you an application which is required to fill to make the online banking account. Once this is done they will provide the username and password of the account. You can access your account using their bank websites like COMBANK or HSBC. And also there are such banks that do not require to visit their bank branch to make the online account. A user can make their account through the internet by an opening website like Payooner or Nettler.

Online banking is very secure; you will get a notification to your phone anytime that you logged in to your account. And also for every transaction that you had done.
