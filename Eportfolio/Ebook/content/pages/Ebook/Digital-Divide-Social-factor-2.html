Social Factor (Cont.)


Locally

Due to the various cultures and religious people they have different attitudes to technology. In Islam religion, they oppose their believers to use technology because they think it may affect the regulations of Islam religion.

Most of the elders think that technology is always bad and teach their children’s not to use technology because they don’t have a good picture about what the technology is and what can it do. Thus many numbers of students are not using internet for their daily work because they don’t have permission from their elders to use the technology, as a result, most of the students are not confident to use the technology.

A good teacher is needed to teach information technology for the students but there are few teachers in some school so it affects children’s education. However now a day most of the high school students use technological devices like smartphones, tabs in order to make their daily studies easy. But on the other hand, a massive number of students use internet only for chatting and to visit unwanted websites.

Uneducated parents provide internet connection to their children without blocking unwanted sites that affect their children studies. This is because they don’t know how to use technology.

Sri Lankan government conduct a survey and found there are active sim cards twice the population of Sri Lanka and most of them are used by people below the age of 18.
