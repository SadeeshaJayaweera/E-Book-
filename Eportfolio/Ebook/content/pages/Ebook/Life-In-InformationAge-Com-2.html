Email can be taken as one of the most common services used by many people worldwide neglecting their profession. It allows us to send thousands of messages just within in few seconds. People in the present world tend to use email service due to the enormous benefits that it provides. In early decades’ individuals faced many problems in using regular mail system. As an example, it takes a huge time to be delivered and get a reply. Thus from 21st-century rapid changes being happened to the communication sector. Most of the companies and organizations used this service to send and receive their relevant documents to the employees, clients and customers. Most of the people use their smartphone to check emails as it easy and fast since the mobile is always with them.

  • With all the good comes bad. Although the emails do helps us lot but there are some issues, many email users received a lot of spam emails which tries to steal user’s details. This is a major problem facing when using email service. Sometimes viruses also come with some emails due to these reasons email service is not very secure so user must be careful in using this service. However, if the user is able to avoid these issues then the user is able to get the full amount from this service.
