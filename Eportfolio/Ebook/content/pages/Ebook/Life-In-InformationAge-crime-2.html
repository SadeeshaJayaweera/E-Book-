Life in the Information Age

Crime And Crime Prevention

  • Mapping Technology
    • Geographic information system and remote sensing applications are allowing law enforcers to make big leaps in criminal intelligence, emerge response and policing.
    • Mapping uses real-time information to access crimes which are happening, to what types of crimes they are. They can identify hot spots and patters of activity, which lets them focus on investigations and improve crime prevention.
  • Smartphone apps
    • A few years ago, we hardly had any apps on our ’s smartphone. Much less any related to crime prevention or reporting. Nowadays people use their phones to take pictures of criminal activities and to text anonymous tips to police. This use of self-reporting will only increase as mobile apps are developed.
    • There are new apps made to look home security with the use of CCTV facility. This is a major revolution in this sector. Majority of people use this service to look at their house’s security and prevent crimes.
  • Web reporting
    • As there are mobile devices for people to report criminal activity. Somehow there are few people who don’t use mobile phones. However, they have the ability to report a criminal’s activities with the use of web browsers from anywhere in public places. Therefore, they can submit anonymous tips to the local police department. It's one way to empower the community to a proactive part of a solution.
