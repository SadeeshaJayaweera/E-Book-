Life in the Information Age

Education

Education

  • The job, which has done by technology in the educational sector is absolutely extraordinary. Most of the highly distinguished educators, philanthropists, and policymakers have perceived that technology is the loophole to educate almost everyone in the world equally. A discussion can take place regarding the mentioned aspect in the following sub-categories: availability of knowledge, self-learning platforms, and online teaching facilities.

Availability of knowledge

  • It’s indeed possible to say that technology has bought all the knowledge around the world to a global virtual library, which can be accessed by any human, who’s having an internet connection. Which means information on any subject, or any sort of book published in the world, an elaborative video series of any subject is open for anyone in the world in a matter of seconds.
