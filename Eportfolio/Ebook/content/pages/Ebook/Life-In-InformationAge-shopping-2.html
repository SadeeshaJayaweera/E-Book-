Life in the Information Age

Shopping (Cont.)

I have been done online shopping so I have a good overview of it. In my conclusion, it is easy and convenient to purchase a product online rather than going to a regular shop. It takes huge time in a physical store to find a product, but within a couple of seconds, we can find a product that we want by using online shopping. In my experience, online shopping provides various category products so it enables us to purchase everything from one place. Regular shopping is not like that it wastes our time and travel cost because it’s not provide everything from just one marketplace.

However, some people are not using online shopping due to various reasons. One is the user is not able to touch the product physically and check the quality of the product. Therefore, most of the elders are not using this service. Online shopping is done using credit cards so sometimes bank account can be hacked so due to these reasons some people are not familiar with the online shopping process.
