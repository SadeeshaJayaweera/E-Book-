Life in the Information Age

Communication (Cont.)


Majority of people in the present world using social networking platforms like facebook and twitter to upload pictures, videos and update statues daily. This service makes people up to date as it provides new information and news to the users. This service enables users to keep in touch with their family members and friends. Most of the teenagers in age 14- 18 use even one social network platform. Some people prefer to use various social network sites as they are different from one to one and the services they provide may differ. When regarding the social network, it is important to talk about business organizations and employees. They are making groups for their employees so everyone can be added so this way they can contact every member altogether. Due to the rapid development of the social network platforms, user is able to install their preferred social media app to their mobile. Third party sellers advertise their product through social networks. So social network provides many advantages to people in different professions.
