Entertainment

Online Books

Online books are known as eBooks. A user can read them using the internet. In modern society, most of the people are using eBooks rather than using the paper book.

However, there are a majority of web sites offer free online books to users. And also software's like "Amazon_Kindle" can be installed into the smartphone, so a user can read books much quicker.

Due to the revolution of the eBook, most of the people in modern society are not familiar with paper books, as they cost high.
