Life in the Information Age

Banking

There are also many banking facilities like Payoneer and nettler which completely work through the online. Thus it provides an ability to the user to do everything online. I have good knowledge of this service as I used this service many times when I was working as a Freelancer. It helps me to get payments easily from overseas clients without paying taxes. They provided me a debit card which can be used from anywhere in the world. Tremendous changes are being happened to the banking sector due to the rapid development of technology.

Although the banking platforms are very secure but it can’t be guaranteed every time because there are many hackers who always tries to steal someone’s account information. There are also some people that like to have face to face communication with the banker so this service is not very compatible with them because online banking doesn’t provide it.

“Fifty-one percent of U.S. adults, or 61% of internet users, bank online. Thirty-two percent of U.S. adults, or 35% of cell phone owners, bank using their mobile phones.” (statistics taken from pewinternet.com)
