Web Storage

Drop Box

This is a cloud service that allows users to back up their data's and share with others online.

Dropbox is available for smartphone and pcs. However, this service is not almost free because they provide few gigabytes for the free version. A user can expand their storage up to Terabytes by expanding their free package to a paid one.

User can use this service by downloading the app or visiting their official website. Once you made an account by providing information, you can use this service from any device by logging into the account you created.

This service is very effective these days because most of the people lost their devices with a lot of information's, so this method helps them to store and manage their documents.
