Entertainment

Online Sports

Online sports service enables a user to have the scores of live matches and previous matches with brief information. You can use this service by visiting a sports web site like CBS Sports, LiveScore, Yahoo Sports, etc...And also this is a free service, you can have the scores anywhere from the earth until you have internet access.

So you can use this service to determine the sports results without taking a visit to the matches.

Some websites provide live preview of present matches so user can watch matches live from their comfort zone, however this is a good service for people in overseas.
