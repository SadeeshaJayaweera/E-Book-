Life in the Information Age

Education (Cont.)


Self-learning platforms

  • If we dig for the deepest aspects of technology on education, we’d see that it’s possible to anyone in the world to tend on self-learning in an organized way on any subject, or preparation towards most of the aptitude tests using online services for free. The best example for this topic is the Khan Academy, which is an American non-profit organization, which provides free education on a number of major subjects, and major exam preparations for anyone in the world for free.

Online teaching facilities

  • That’s the next hot topic of the in this mentioned aspect. With the development of technology and internet, the ICT has taken up the world into a new level, where anyone in the world with knowledge in an applicable quality can tend on teaching on their subjects, which they have expertise, and make money accordingly. Udemy and Skillshare are perfect examples for that.
