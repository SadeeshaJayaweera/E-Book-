E-Commerce

Online Shopping

In order to shop through the online, you were required to open up an online website like eBay or aliexpress.when this is finished you must make an account by giving your personal details including your address, name, etc...After that, you can choose a product that you hope to purchase. And after selecting the product, It must be added to the cart. If you choose more than one product you can add them to the cart altogether. When you have picked your items you are ready to move to the checkout. Once the customer paid for the product using the credit card, the order will be confirmed and you can have tracking details to estimate delivery.
