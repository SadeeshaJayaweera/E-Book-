Entertainment and Leisure (Cont.)

When talking about leisure and entertainment it is must to be talk about video gaming. In the current society most of the people prefer to play games using Laptops, PlayStation and even smartphones. However it provide some kind of an exercise to the users but it’s not good at all the time. when reflecting to previous years’ people went to play grounds and played physically with others. But in current society majority of teenager are stepped away from physical sports and tend to play computer games continuously.

Video gaming is very attractive because I was also played many games in the last two years but it’s not good because it causes many health problems for me. I have better knowledge about gaming and how to use it perfectly because it had affected me in many ways. However, gaming helps us to make decisions quickly in the real world. Now there are many video games available for many devices so playing a game is not hard for anyone in this world. Therefore, this is a very good service for busy people who don’t have time to visit playgrounds or Gymnasium to have exercises.

In my conclusion gaming is not good for school students because the majority of students in my school playing computer games daily without studying or even taking their regular meals. some of them failed in their exams due to overtime gaming. However, gaming is expensive in sometimes because some new games required good system requirements.
