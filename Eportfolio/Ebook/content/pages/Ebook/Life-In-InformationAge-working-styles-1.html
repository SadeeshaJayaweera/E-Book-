Life in the Information Age

Working Styles

ICT has affected human life in an extreme way when it comes to the working world. The way of handling office work can be emphasized as the best example, which can be comparatively studied with the previous century.

When reflecting the past of forty years before, the offices were filled up with mountains of files with plenty of documents, which should be analysed with the human brain power with a firm effort. But now, in the present day working environment, someone would barely see a desk like that. Instead of a view of a well-organized desk with nothing; but a laptop, or a tab, or perhaps barely a desktop would be seen.

In the present day, people tend to use word processors like Microsoft Word, Google docs to conveniently tend on drafting documents, letters while decreasing the time consumption, but increasing efficiency and productivity. In past accountants used typewriters to wrote letters. Main disadvantage in this is, if you want to make changes or resurrection to the typed letter then you have to retype it from the beginning. However, this machine has replaced with the computer. Thus it enables people to make changes to their letters efficiently.

There are many jobs roles being created because applications like spreadsheets, word processing are being used for data analyzing and modelling process in almost every company. In banks, there are specialist ICT teams to maintain databases and networks. as a result, a wide range of jobs is available for the people.
