The Digital Divide

Introduction

The gap between the ability and the inability of the people using technology is called digital divide. This gap exists due to different reasons. As the main reason, we can consider the lack of knowledge in using technologically advanced devices. Other than that socioeconomic background of the people emerges as a substantial topic regarding this concern.

The lack of knowledge in using technologically advanced devices exists mostly due to the inability of people older people on learning new technology. It exists because when human beings grow old, they are generally being deprived of acquiring knowledge on new arenas. In the same way, the students, who are being cultivated in geographies with conservative education systems; and that reason exists because such geographies are incapable of achieving the economic conditions, which required on building technology-based communities.

So, this is a comprehensive study regarding how such reasons are being discussed locally and globally.
